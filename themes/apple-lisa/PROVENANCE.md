## About

The Apple Lisa (1983) was Apple's predecessor to the Macintosh — an all-in-one desktop with the first commercial mouse-driven GUI in their product line. The Lisa Office System established many of the conventions (menu bar, drag-to-move windows, the appearance of scroll bars) that the Macintosh inherited. This scheme retargets classic Mac OS Appearance chrome to mimic the Lisa Office System B/W interface.

*"Apple Lisa colour scheme(s) for Kaleidoscope 2.1 or later. A scheme by R. Bensam and E. Deans. Based on Apple Lisa colour scheme(s) for Kaleidoscope 1.5.x - 1.7.x, by E. Deans. Apple Lisa is a rendition of the Lisa Desktop Interface found in the Lisa Office System, the system software of the predecessor to Macintosh."* From the bundle's ReadMe.
