## About

Floppies! is a Kaleidoscope scheme drawing chrome and icons in the style of the period's 3.5" diskettes (the "in MY day..." PICT companion suggests a retro-on-retro joke even at the time).

No plaintext ReadMe in the archive — the only companion is a PICT image ("in MY day...") with no extractable license text. The scheme was distributed via the period Kaleidoscope community as freeware-with-redistribution by convention.
