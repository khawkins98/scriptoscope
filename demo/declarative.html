About Aaron

Aaron is a window manager for the World Wide Web. It draws faithful Mac OS 8.5 windows around ordinary HTML — text, forms, images, and links — without modifying that content. Everything inside this window is real, selectable DOM.

What's new in this version

  • Window-shade by double-clicking a title bar.
  • Click the zoom box to grow a window to fit its content.
  • Themed scrollbars on windows whose content overflows.
  • The Inspector re-skins every window live.

Getting started

Drag the title bar of any window to move it. Drag the gripper at the bottom-right to resize. Click a window to bring it forward; the chrome switches from inactive to active.
